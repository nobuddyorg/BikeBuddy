      How to use BikeBuddy

      🧭 What is this?

      Upload GPX tracks from your rides — cycling or motorcycling — and see them as an interactive heatmap. Warmer colours mean you rode there more often.

      🔑 Sign in

      Sign in to upload tours and have them saved to your account.

      ⬆️ Upload a tour

      Click Upload GPX, give it a name, and drop a .gpx file. It appears on the map straight away.

      📋 Your tours

      The sidebar lists your tours. Use the search box to filter and the dropdown to sort by date, name, or length. Click a tour to focus it; Show All Tours brings back the combined heatmap.

      📷 Photos & pins

      Open a tour to add photos, edit, or delete it. Toggle Photo pins on the map to show geotagged photos where they were taken.
